About Me

I solve technology problems without making users feel small.

I am an IT Systems Technician supporting more than 300 users and over 1,400 technology assets across eight healthcare locations, including mobile units. My experience includes Windows endpoint deployment, Microsoft 365, healthcare device integration, asset management, wireless infrastructure support, and user onboarding.

My strongest value is the bridge I create between users and technical teams. I listen carefully, explain clearly, document accurately, and escalate with useful evidence when an issue requires higher-level access.
